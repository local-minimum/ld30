Little Beatle Saves The World

How to play:

You, , must make it to while avoiding before you run out of using only (WASD works too) and your wit.

Press to mute the music.

Press / to pause (get to the menu).

The world has separated onto three planes. Scattered piecies of what once was a comforting and predictable whole have become a dangerous place to live in. There are still a few connections between the emerging worlds. Just enough to traverse the multi-world to reach the beacon that can bring them all together again.
